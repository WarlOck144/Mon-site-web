Caratéristique Personnelles

My name is Oscar Bouriot. I am 17 years old. I live in the center of Toulouse, near Francois Verdier station. I have 2 older brothers named Charles and Antoine.


Last year, I was a student at Saint Joseph La Salle High School in Toulouse, where I studied STI2D, which stands for Science and Technologies of Industry and Sustainable Development. My focus was on IT Systems and Digital Engineering.


As for my personality, I would describe myself as patient and optimistic. I believe that these qualities are essential in both my personal life and my studies, helping me face challenges calmly and with a positive mindset.

Centre d'intérêt

I’m very passionate about snowboarding. Every year, I go snowboarding in the mountains with my two older brothers. Being in the mountains is like entering another world. It’s a place where I feel completely free and at peace.

Activité Extra-scolaire

One of my biggest passions is boxing. I’ve been practicing it for four years, and I’ve had the honor of becoming the Occitanie regional champion twice. Boxing is a sport that requires a lot of discipline, and it has taught me how to stay focused and determined.

Choix BUT R&T

I chose BUT because it's the closest to where I live, as well as having a good reputation and convincing results.


I specifically chose the Networks and Telecommunications (R&T) BUT because I was initially hesitating between computer science and networks and telecoms. However, during the open house, I realized that the computer science program was mainly focused on coding, which doesn't really interest me. What's more, from the second year onwards, the R&T department offers a course in cybersecurity, a field that particularly appeals to me.
